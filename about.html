Non-profit charity organization

Welcome to Meal Connect, a revolutionary platform that bridges the gap between hotels and NGOs to combat food waste and fight hunger. Our mission is to utilize the surplus food from hotels and distribute it to underprivileged individuals, ensuring that no meal goes to waste. At Meal Connect, we understand the pressing issue of food waste and its detrimental impact on our society and the environment. Meanwhile, we also acknowledge the alarming number of people who struggle with hunger and malnutrition. To address this dual challenge, we have created an innovative online platform that seamlessly connects hotels and NGOs, fostering a collaborative effort to make a positive change.
